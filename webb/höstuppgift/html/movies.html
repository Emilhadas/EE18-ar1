Three Halloween Movies

Halloween

Halloween is a American slasher movie from 2018. The movie is about a halloween celebration taking place in the us. But with a white masked man that hunt people. A short recap of what the movie is about without getting into so many spoilers. If you dare to watch it.

Frankenstein

Victor Frankenstein is the main character from the 2015 Victor Frankenstein. The movie is about a scietist that tries to bring alive the dead, by making a "human" out of dead human parts. With his assistans Igor.

Van Helsing

Van Helsing is an American horror/action movie from 2004. The movie is about a monster hunter, called Van Helsing, that's there to help a lady to fight down dracula and save her brother.
